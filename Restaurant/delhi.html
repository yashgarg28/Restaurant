SEVILLA

There are not many Spanish restaurants in New Delhi and not as beautiful as Sevilla at the claridges. This restaurant is perfectly made for special occasions. The design is breathtakingly beautiful. This scenic place is so romantic it builds anticipation the moment you start walking on the cobbled path leading to the restaurant. Instead of one room restaurant you will be delighted to come upon little pavilions and covered areas serving as the restaurant. The outdoor and indoor blending together Sevilla will enchant you endlessly. The stunning concept of pavilions and covered areas with lighted cobbled path transports you to Spain. The restaurant is cleverly woven with the trees and the upper floor sitting area on the pavilion makes it a heady memory. The cuisine served at Sevilla is Mediterranean with major influence from Spanish territory. The food as expected of a five star hotel is so delicious and lovely.

Le Cirque

Savour French-Italian cuisine in settings that make you want to come back for more. Make an experience out of dining - try chef's tasting menu that will embark you on an inspiring culinary journey. It’s the most sought after fine-dining restaurant in Delhi, hosting many celebrity guests, socialites and dignitaries from across the world. Le Cirque, the grand landmark restaurant of New York brings its avant-garde bar with exclusive walk-in wine cellar, an interactive chef’s table, Delhi’s most prestigious show kitchen and exclusive private dining rooms. Dishes that our chef recommends include Le Cirque Garden Vegetables with Truffle Dressing, Ravioli stuffed with Barolo Braised Duck Leg with Black Truffle and Paupiette of Chilean Sea Bass.

Varq

One of the first to hone the concept of pre-plated Indian food, the award winning Varq purveys a modern Indian gourmet dining experience offering authenticity of taste and artistic presentation. The menu engages with all the five senses with visually stunning dishes, delight on the palate and aromas of Indian spices and promises to set a benchmark for contemporary Indian cuisine. The restaurant proudly houses priceless artwork - a mural by renowned artist Anjolie Ela Menon and the modern art forms of oil paintings, carvings and sculptures.
